prompt: |
  Help me plan a family trip. Ask me one question at a time: where we're going and when, who's coming (ages matter), our rough budget, what kind of trip we want (packed vs. relaxed), and any must-dos or deal-breakers.

  Then build a day-by-day plan with one anchor activity per day, backup options for bad weather, realistic meal stops, and downtime built in. Flag anything that needs booking ahead.
---

Trip planning usually dies in seventeen open browser tabs. This prompt makes the AI act like a travel agent who asks good questions first — so the plan fits your actual family instead of a fantasy one.

## How to use it

1. Copy the prompt into ChatGPT or Claude.
2. Be honest in your answers ("my 6-year-old melts down after 3pm" is useful data).
3. Ask for changes like you would with a person: "day two is too packed," "swap the museum for something outdoors."

**Try next:** once the plan looks right, ask for a packing list based on the itinerary and the weather.
